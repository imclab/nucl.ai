Conference Tracks

Generally speaking, nucl.ai is made up of nine day-long tracks on specific topics, with three running in parallel each day over three days. The current topics are as follows, and will be finalized by the first week of May when single-day tickets become available:

{% include page/tracks/header.html %}

Each of these tracks consists of an inspiring keynote and/or presentations from high-profile applications, specialized masterclasses and in-depth tutorials, panel discussions and expert Q&A, and (if applicable) an open laboratory in workshop-style. The different types of sessions are scheduled in different rooms throughout the day.
